Goals (Mission)

My goal is to build a career in software development and design, making technology accessible and understandable for everyone. I believe in the power of design to bridge the gap between complex technology and everyday users. I aspire to participate in creating useful, efficient systems that enhance daily life. By combining thoughtful design and innovative technology, I aim to improve user experiences and contribute to developing solutions that are both practical and impactful.

Purpose (Vision)

My purpose is to use technology and clever design to connect people with their loved ones, family, and friends. I aim to enhance everyone's lives by making meaningful connections easier and more enriching. I believe technology is a powerful tool, and it is our duty to use it as efficiently as possible to bring out the best in human creativity and capacities.

Meet Me

My name is Alison Talbot. Costa Rica born and raised, I currently reside in the USA with my beloved husband.

My passion for technology and design began early, inspired by my father, a web developer. This early exposure to design, art, and technology guided me towards a career in software development. Growing up with my traditional and Catholic grandparents instilled in me strong values and a deep sense of purpose. These influences have shaped my career, where I now focus on crafting efficient and innovative software solutions.

My Projects

Medical Center Appointments Project

I worked on a project with my class teammates to create an appointment scheduling system for a medical center. This system managed patients, registered and administered doctors and services, and handled multiple branches. The project was developed in Visual Studio Community using C# and .NET, utilizing an API with a cloud database on Azure and hosted locally. Although the project is no longer running, it was a great learning experience for me, to understand how to produce a whole project from back to front end.
